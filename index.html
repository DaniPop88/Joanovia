ABOUT JOANOVIA URBAN BOUTIQUE
Joanovia Urban Boutique is a proudly Filipino-owned boutique based in Quezon City, NCR. We specialize in providing fashionable, high-quality apparel and accessories designed for the modern Filipina.

From street style to elegant evening wear, our curated selection is perfect for any occasion. Whether you need a chic look for a night out or a comfy dress for daily wear, we have you covered.

Our Mission: To empower and inspire every customer to express their individuality through fashion.
